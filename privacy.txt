Privacy policy

General

"Notes" is developed by one main developer, with a few people helping from time to time, mostly with translations.

Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how information obtained from users is collected, used and disclosed.

By using "Notes", you agree that your personal information will be handled as described in this Policy.

The app has no internet access, so it is not sending anything outside the device whatsoever.

Information being collected

"Notes" does not collect any personal identifiable information.

The WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permission is used on Android below 8 for accessing, editing or removing files containing notes created by users.
